Beatrice Boadi

CEO of Bite n Bloom

Early Life and Education:

Beatrice Boadi was born in the Central Region specifically Agona Bobikuma, Ghana on October 12, 2003. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology Management from the University of Professional Studies, Accra and a MBA from Yale School of Management.

Career:

She started influencing at an early age and became well known across social media in 2015. She became the owner of her company in 2020 and made it popular across several countries. During her tenure, she oversaw significant growth and transformation of Bite n Bloom, focusing on better food, tidy restaurant and better services. She was also known for her strong leadership and strategic thinking, and for her advocacy for women in business. She invested in other businesses like a boutique, a massage palour, a salon and many others.

Bite n Bloom:

Bite n Bloom is a conceptual restaurant that merges contemporary aesthetics with an earthy, organic charm. The interior design features a harmonious mix of sleek marble finishes and rustic wooden accents, evoking a sense of modern sophistication and natural warmth. Carefully curated ambient lighting—through pendant lights and soft chandeliers—enhances the cozy and inviting atmosphere. The restaurant’s layout incorporates both private seating nooks and communal spaces, catering to diverse dining experiences. The name "Bite n Bloom" reflects the restaurant’s ethos: offering exquisite culinary creations that inspire growth and connection, much like the flourishing of a garden. This concept exemplifies the blending of elegance, comfort, and creativity in modern dining.
